Biography

The fight itself towards the summits suffices to fill a heart of man.

Siwei Yang is a first-year Ph.D. student at UCSC supervised by Prof. Cihang Xie.

He received his Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science at Tongji University, Shanghai, China. He was an research assistant supervised by Prof. Yin Wang at Tongji University. After graduation, he has worked as a visiting research assistant at MMLab@HKUST under the supervision of Prof. Dan Xu and a research summer intern at CCVL@JHU under the supervision of Prof. Alan Yuille and Prof. Hang Zhao.
